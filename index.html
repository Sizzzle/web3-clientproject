Our passion

Current, regular and seasonably available beers

Rollo Pale Wheat

Our house session beer brewed with white wheat and generously dry hopped with Equinox, El Dorado, and Citra hops. Light, bright and aroma for miles

Lnk common

A warm fermented lager produced with a nod to history by using only the finest malt and Northern Brewer hops: Year round availability.

Inverted Galaxy

A modern, more balanced and aromatic Double IPA brewed with an unnecessarily large amount of Australian Galaxy Hops: Seasonal Availability.

Bier De Garde

Our traditional Amber Biere De Garde brewed with French malt and hops then conditioned with just a touch of our Brettanomyces and Lacto Bacillus blend to develop in the bottle for years to come.

Saison

Classic belgian saison brewed with just a touch of wheat, a healthy dose of strisselspalt hops and our house saison yeast: Year round availability

Skinny legs IPA

Traditional notes of an American IPA, juicy stone fruit, pine resin and citrus; minimal malt backbone and drying effervescence leaves our IPA clean and crisp.

Grisette

Petite Saison brewed with fruity American hops. A saison lovers session beer; soft and light with a touch of yeast and bright stone fruit.

Single Hop Farmhouse

An imperial take on our farmhouse ale with a large does of American or Australian hops. This series will rotate throughout the year.

Who WE Are

with a clever little tagline to go with it here

White Elm Brewing was built around a shared passion for handcrafted ales and a willingness to defy logic and pursue our love. With a focus on using only the highest quality, vegan friendly ingredients and a reverence for brewing history, we offer our modern American and classic Belgian ales to our community in Southeast Nebraska.
