We Brew True

It's the White Elm way

We don't filter, we don't re-hydrate, we don't mechanically cut corners. We don't do anything to 'speed up' the natural brewing process. The result: quality, one of brews.

The Brew Crew

Tasty Suds for you & your buds

Rollo Pale Wheat

Our house session beer brewed with white wheat and generously dry hopped with Equinox, El Dorado, and Citra hops. Light, bright and aroma for miles

Farmhouse Ale

Effervescent and light with a tart note and funk that will grow with time. Our house yeast and brettanomyces blend gives this ale its character.

Scotch Ale

First running wort reduction and an extended boil develops a full mouthfeel and notes of vanilla, caramel, tobacco and leather. Big, chewy and rich.

Bier De Garde

Our traditional Amber Biere De Garde brewed with French malt and hops then conditioned with just a touch of our Brettanomyces and Lacto Bacillus blend to develop in the bottle for years to come.

Single Hop Farmhouse

An imperial take on our farmhouse ale with a large does of American or Australian hops. This series will rotate throughout the year.

Skinny legs IPA

Traditional notes of an American IPA, juicy stone fruit, pine resin and citrus; minimal malt backbone and drying effervescence leaves our IPA clean and crisp.

Grisette

Petite Saison brewed with fruity American hops. A saison lovers session beer; soft and light with a touch of yeast and bright stone fruit.

About White Elm

Who are these people and why is their beer so good?

White Elm Brewing Company strives to bring modern takes on rustic ales to southeast Nebraska. We love funk; farmhouse styles, saisons, grisette and biere de garde find their way through our fermenters on a regular basis. Between brewing our rustic ales we weave Scotch Ales, ESB’s, and loads of hoppy ales through our brew schedule. Look for cans of White Elm Brewing Company’s beers to continue hit shelves throughout the Lincoln and Omaha Metro areas.
